Risk Management Tool

This tool helps small businesses assess the risk level of their key assets by evaluating potential threats and vulnerabilities. It provides a detailed breakdown of risks, mitigation tips, and tracks previous assessments to identify trends.

1. Identify Assets

**Low**: You’re uncertain about the asset’s value or impact.
**Medium**: You have some information but may be making assumptions.
**High**: You’re confident and have reliable information about the asset.

2. Identify Threats

**Low**: You’re uncertain about the threat’s likelihood.
**Medium**: You have some understanding but may lack complete data.
**High**: You’re confident, backed by reliable sources or experience.

3. Identify Vulnerabilities

**Low**: You’re unsure about the vulnerability’s impact or relevance.
**Medium**: You’re somewhat confident but could use more information.
**High**: You’re very certain about the vulnerability’s impact based on solid data.

4. Assess Risk

Click the button to calculate risk level, view recommendations, and see previous assessments.
